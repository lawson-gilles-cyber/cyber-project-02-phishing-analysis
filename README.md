# 🎣 Cyber Project #02 - Phishing Email Analysis

## Context

Phishing attacks are one of the most common cyber threats targeting users.

This project simulates the analysis of a suspicious email to identify phishing indicators.

---

## Objective

* Identify phishing characteristics
* Analyze suspicious email content
* Apply SOC investigation methodology

---

## 📧 Suspicious Email

```
From: support@paypaI.com
Subject: Urgent: Account Verification Required

Dear user,

We detected unusual activity on your account.
Please verify your account immediately:

http://paypal-secure-login.com
```

---

## Analysis

### 1. Suspicious Sender

* "paypaI.com" uses a capital "I" instead of "l"

### 2. Urgency

* Creates pressure to act quickly

### 3. Suspicious Link

* Fake domain not belonging to PayPal

### 4. Generic Greeting

* "Dear user" instead of personalized name

---

## Conclusion

This email is a **phishing attempt**.

---

## 🛡️ Recommendations

* Do not click on links
* Verify sender authenticity
* Report the email

---

## Key Takeaways

* Recognizing phishing indicators
* Understanding social engineering
* SOC analysis basics
